COLOR PICKER - Desktop only

My partner has something called synesthesia. For their specific kind, they can look at black printed letters or numbers and see colors associated with each one. I thought it would be fun to think about what we all associate certain letters with, synesthete or not. Click start on the button below and then select whatever color you feel best represents, then scroll down to start typing with the colors you chose.
